how do i make them??

  1. bring those steaks to room tempterature. long enough for you to worry about food poisoning
  2. heat the grill to medium heat. season the steaks and grill them until 5-7 minutes on each side. dont put the grill to close to the couch
  3. remove them steaks. dot?? them with butter. and let them take a nap for about 10 minutes
